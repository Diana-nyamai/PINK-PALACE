Welcome to The Pink House, a picturesque photography location in Runda,is set amidst blooming gardens, on the hilltops of the Karura Forest.

EVENT

GARDENS IN RUNDA

The gardens are the perfect location for that special photo shoot, be it a wedding, an engagement, a family shoot or a baby bump shoot. Complete with a sparkling swimming pool, water fountains and rustic benches sprawled all over the gardens. There is more than enough spots to make beautiful memories!

WEDDING PHOTO SESSION

A wedding is a once in a lifetime moment and when the pictures matter most, you need to choose the best photo shoot location. Come make memories with your spouse and bridal party at Karura House.We have all the features to make it a day to remember!

FAMILY SHOOT

The grounds are perfect for pictures with your little ones and loved ones. With various locations to capture stunning shots, you may never go back to the studio for your family portraits again!

ENGAGEMENT SHOOT

Celebrate the fact that she said yes with photos that you will look back upon years to come. Bring your fiancé/fiancée and begin your engagement on a sparkly note. We are happy to help you organize any special surprises.
